Site Name:

LF travel

This name was chosen to identify the name of the travel agency, LF from "Leticia Figueiredo" and Travel to identify that we work with travel.

Site Purpose:

The site promotes a secure environment where customers can contact travel consultants to organize their trip or buy the desired products directly. Customers will also have access to the company's blog where there will be updates on the travel consultants' trips.

Scenarios:

What is the best time of the year to go to Spain?
What is the best destination to a family trip?
